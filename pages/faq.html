Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SkillCircle and how to make the most of it.

Do I need to create an account?

No! SkillCircle is completely free and requires no account creation. Just fill out the post form with your information and you're ready to go. All your posts are stored locally in your browser.

Is SkillCircle really free?

Yes! SkillCircle is 100% free. There are no fees, no payments, no commissions. It runs entirely in your browser and we take nothing. Skill exchanges are direct human-to-human — no middleman.

How do I post a skill?

Go to the Browse Skills page and fill out the post form at the top. Share your name, location, what you can teach, what you need help with, and a brief description. Choose a category and whether you're offering or requesting. Click "Post to the circle" and you're live!

Can I delete or edit my post?

Currently, posts are stored locally in your browser. If you want to remove a post, you can clear your browser's localStorage. In a future version, we'll add the ability to manage and edit your posts directly through the interface.

How do I start a skill exchange?

Find someone whose skills interest you, click the "Connect" button, write them a friendly message introducing yourself and your interests, and send it. They'll see your message and can respond. From there, you discuss terms, format, and schedule directly with each other.

Is my information secure?

All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage — it never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. Only the information you choose to share publicly (in your post) is visible to others. Use a pseudonym or first name if you prefer privacy.

Can I search for specific skills?

Absolutely! Use the search box to find skills by keyword. You can also use the filter buttons to browse by type (offerings/requests) or by category (Tech, Arts, Language, etc.). Combine search and filters to find exactly what you're looking for.

What happens after I send a connection message?

Your message is recorded as a connection. The other person will see you've connected. Currently, SkillCircle shows you that a connection was made, but actual messaging happens outside the platform. We recommend exchanging contact info (email, phone, messaging app) in your first message to continue the conversation.
