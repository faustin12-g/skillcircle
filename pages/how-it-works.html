How SkillCircle Works

A simple, transparent guide to posting your skills, finding matches, and starting exchanges.

Step 1: Post Your Skills

Visit the Browse Skills page and fill out the post form. Tell the community:

  • Your name and location
  • What skill you can offer
  • What skill you need help with
  • A brief description about yourself
  • Choose a category that best fits
  • Whether you're offering or requesting

Step 2: Browse & Filter

Once your post is live, you can start browsing other community members:

  • Use filters to find specific skills or categories
  • Search by skill name or person's name
  • View their offering and needs
  • Read their stories and background

Step 3: Send a Connection

Found someone whose skills you need? Click "Connect" on their post:

  • Compose a friendly message
  • Introduce yourself and your interests
  • Express why you're interested in the exchange
  • Send your message

Step 4: Start Your Exchange

Once someone connects with you:

  • Discuss terms and schedules
  • Decide on format: online, in-person, async
  • Set expectations and goals
  • Start learning together!

Tips for Success

  • Be specific: The more details you provide, the better matches you'll find.
  • Be responsive: Reply quickly to connection requests.
  • Be respectful: Honor agreed-upon schedules and commitments.
  • Be patient: Finding the right match takes time sometimes.
  • Be honest: About your skill level and availability.
  • Be flexible: Be open to different formats and approaches.
